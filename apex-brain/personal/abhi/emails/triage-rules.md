# Abhi - Email Triage Rules

Rules for AI-assisted email management across all accounts.

## General Rules (all accounts)
1. Flag anything requiring Abhi's personal decision or signature
2. Mary handles scheduling replies -- forward calendar-related emails to her
3. Never auto-reply to external contacts without approval
4. Archive newsletters and promotional emails after scanning for relevant content

## NLC Work Email (abhi@neurolegalconsulting.com)

### Priority: Immediate
- New case referrals from law firms
- Existing case updates or urgent attorney requests
- Physician availability responses

### Priority: Same Day
- Law firm relationship outreach responses
- Invoice or payment-related emails
- Credentialing or compliance items

### Priority: Weekly Review
- Marketing and conference invitations
- Vendor or tool provider emails
- General industry newsletters

### Auto-Actions
- Label new case referral emails as "New Case"
- Label payment/invoice emails as "Finance"
- Label physician correspondence as "Physician Network"

## Personal Gmail

### Priority: Immediate
- Medical or financial institution correspondence
- Family/personal urgent items

### Priority: Weekly Review
- Subscriptions, newsletters
- Promotional emails

### Auto-Actions
- Archive promotional/marketing emails after 7 days
- Label financial items as "Finance"
