# Abhi Kapuria - Personal Dashboard

## Purpose
Personal life management page. Tasks, todos, and items that don't belong to any specific business entity.

## Categories
- **Credentialing:** Medical license renewals, board certifications, hospital privileges
- **Personal tasks:** Appointments, errands, personal projects
- **Finance:** Personal investment tracking, tax deadlines
- **Health:** Appointments, wellness tracking
- **Email:** Personal and work email management (see emails/)

## Subfolders
- `tasks/` - Active personal tasks and recurring items
- `emails/` - Email account configs and management rules
- `credentialing/` - License and certification tracking
- `finance/` - Personal financial items separate from business entities
