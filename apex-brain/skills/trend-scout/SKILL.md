# Trend Scout

Monitor trending topics, tools, and opportunities across industries.

## What It Does
- Scan GitHub trending, Product Hunt, Trendshift
- Monitor industry-specific news (medical-legal, fintech, local services)
- Identify emerging investment opportunities for A2Z
- Spot content trends for social media timing
- Weekly digest of relevant trends per entity

## Inputs
- Entity name (or "all" for cross-entity scan)
- Industry focus
- Timeframe (daily, weekly)

## Outputs
- Trend digest with relevance scores
- Actionable recommendations per entity
- Saved to outputs/[entity]/trends/
