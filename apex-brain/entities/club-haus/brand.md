# Club Haus Brand Guide

## Voice
Fresh, confident, community-driven. A barbershop that feels like a club.

## Tone
- Social: trendy, visual-first, aspirational
- Website: clean, modern, easy to book
- Onboarding docs: professional but welcoming

## Do
- Lean into the "parlor" premium feel
- Target the GSU crowd with relevant aesthetics
- Show the space, the vibe, the work

## Don't
- Look like a generic barbershop template
- Over-corporate the messaging
- Ignore the college market positioning
