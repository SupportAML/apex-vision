# Porcupine Edu Brand Guide

## Voice
Friendly, educational, fun. Think National Geographic meets a quirky animal blog.

## Tone
- Informative but not academic
- Light humor welcome
- Accessible to all ages

## Visual Style
- Nature photography focus
- Warm earth tones (browns, greens, amber)
- Clean, modern layout
- Illustrated porcupine mascot for merch

## Do
- Use real facts and cite sources
- Make content shareable and visually engaging
- Feature high-quality porcupine imagery

## Don't
- Be dry or textbook-like
- Ignore mobile experience
- Use stock photos that look fake
