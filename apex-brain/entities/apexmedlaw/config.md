# ApexMedLaw

**Type:** Holding company for medical-legal consulting branches
**Description:** Parent company for NLC and future specialty branches. Each medical specialty gets its own branch under this umbrella.
**Status:** Infancy / structuring
**Branches:**
- Neurology/Neurosurgery (NLC, active)
- Critical Care (launching, one doc already landed a case worth tens of thousands)
- GI (early, couple cases done)

## Growth Strategy
- Each specialty branch recruits its own physicians
- Shared infrastructure: case management tools, law firm relationships, marketing
- Source physicians from SEAK registry, LinkedIn, medical conferences
- The real unlock is getting law firms to see the full ApexMedLaw offering across specialties
