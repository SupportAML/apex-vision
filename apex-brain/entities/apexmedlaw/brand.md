# ApexMedLaw Brand Guide

## Voice
Institutional credibility with startup energy. We're building the largest physician expert witness network, one specialty at a time.

## Tone
- External: "Every specialty. Every case type. One platform."
- To law firms: emphasize breadth of coverage and quality of physician network
- To physicians: emphasize ease of joining and revenue opportunity

## Do
- Present as the umbrella that makes NLC even stronger
- Highlight multi-specialty coverage as a differentiator
- Show the platform/tools as infrastructure that makes it all work

## Don't
- Compete with NLC messaging (NLC is the flagship, ApexMedLaw is the platform)
- Overstate capabilities before branches are actually live
