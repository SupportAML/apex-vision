# AI Influencer Brand Guide

## Visual Identity

### Character Design Spec
- **Ethnicity:** Indian
- **Skin tone:** Warm brown, golden undertone, glowing
- **Hair:** Long, dark brown/black, styled variously (straight, wavy, updos, wet hair looks)
- **Eyes:** Deep brown, expressive, bedroom eyes energy
- **Build:** Slim/athletic, model proportions, curves in the right places
- **Age presentation:** 23-25
- **Distinguishing features:** TBD after LoRA testing (beauty mark, specific eyebrow shape, full lips)
- **Vibe:** The girl who looks effortlessly gorgeous whether she's in a sari or a towel. Confident in her body. Not trying hard, just naturally magnetic.

### Photography Style
- Mix of editorial (Vogue India) and intimate iPhone-selfie aesthetic
- Natural lighting, golden hour, soft morning light, candlelit evenings
- Camera angles that feel personal: mirror selfies, over-the-shoulder, laying in bed POV, close-ups
- Locations: luxury hotel rooms/bathrooms, rooftop pools, beaches, vanity tables, bedrooms, Dubai penthouses, Bali villas
- Color palette: warm golds, deep reds, ivory, black, nude tones, honey lighting
- Skin should glow — dewy, not matte. Looks fresh out of the shower or mid-skincare routine.
- Never: cheap backgrounds, harsh lighting, uncanny valley artifacts, overly posed/stiff

### Content Formats
1. **Reels (primary)** — GRWM, getting dressed, beauty routines, lifestyle vlogs, lip sync, outfit transitions
2. **Feed posts** — Sultry editorial shots, mirror selfies, outfit of the day, "candid" moments
3. **Carousels** — Photo dumps (date night, beach day, travel), beauty routines, outfit inspo
4. **Stories** — Polls ("which outfit?"), Q&A, unfiltered moments, affiliate swipe-ups, "just woke up" content

### Brand Voice
- First person, warm, flirty but classy
- Short, punchy captions with personality. Slightly teasing. Confident.
- Mix of English and occasional Hindi/Urdu phrases for authenticity
- Caption examples:
  - "ready but no one asked me out yet"
  - "the towel did the heavy lifting today"
  - "brown girl summer has no end date"
  - "outfit check or should I just stay in this robe"
- Hashtag strategy: mix of reach (#reels #explorepage #grwm), lifestyle (#luxurylifestyle #ootd), cultural (#indianbeauty #desigirl #browngirl), body-positive (#confident #selflove)
- Never: overly salesy, desperate engagement bait, generic AI-sounding captions, vulgar/explicit

## Content Pillars (Revised Split)

1. **GRWM / Getting Ready / Beauty (35%)** — Skincare routines, doing makeup, hair styling, getting dressed, shower-to-ready transformations. This is the bread and butter. Intimate, personal, sexy-without-trying.
2. **Fashion & Style (25%)** — Outfit transitions, mirror selfies, try-on hauls, "which one?" polls, date night fits
3. **Travel & Lifestyle (20%)** — Luxury hotel content (robe + room service + balcony views), pool/beach days, city walks, restaurant moments
4. **Personal / Thirst Trap / Engagement (20%)** — Candid-looking photos, "photo dump" carousels, confidence/body-positive content, trending audio lip syncs, relatable cultural humor

## The "Sexy but Smart" Line

The account walks the line that top Instagram models walk: suggestive, never explicit on IG.

**Instagram (public, brand-safe):**
- Lingerie/swimwear is fine (it's fashion)
- Towel/robe content is fine (it's GRWM)
- Skin-showing is fine (it's confidence)
- Bedroom/bathroom settings are fine (it's lifestyle)
- Suggestive captions are fine (it's personality)
- Everything stays within Instagram Community Guidelines

**Fanvue (subscription, exclusive):**
- More intimate/exclusive versions of the same content
- Extended GRWM that IG cuts short
- "Unfiltered" behind-the-scenes
- The tease-to-paywall funnel: IG shows the setup, Fanvue shows the exclusive version

## Brand Safety Rules
- All Instagram content stays within IG Community Guidelines (no nudity, no explicit)
- All AI-generated content properly labeled per Meta's policy
- No impersonation of real people
- No political or religious content
- No health/medical claims
- Sexy and confident, never trashy or degrading
- She's in control of the narrative — empowered, not objectified
