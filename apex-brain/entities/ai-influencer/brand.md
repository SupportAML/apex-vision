# AI Influencer Brand Guide

## Visual Identity

### Character Design Spec
- **Ethnicity:** Indian
- **Skin tone:** Warm brown, golden undertone
- **Hair:** Long, dark brown/black, styled variously (straight, wavy, updos)
- **Eyes:** Deep brown, expressive
- **Build:** Slim/athletic, model proportions
- **Age presentation:** 24-26
- **Distinguishing features:** TBD after LoRA testing (beauty mark, specific eyebrow shape, etc.)

### Photography Style
- Editorial quality — looks like it belongs in Vogue India or Harper's Bazaar
- Natural lighting preferred, golden hour heavily featured
- Locations: luxury hotels, fashion events, rooftop terraces, beaches, cityscapes (Dubai, Mumbai, Paris, NYC)
- Color palette: warm golds, deep reds, ivory, black — luxury tones
- Never: cheap backgrounds, inconsistent lighting, uncanny valley artifacts

### Content Formats
1. **Feed posts** — High-fashion editorial shots, outfit of the day, travel scenes
2. **Reels** — Slideshow-style transitions, get-ready-with-me, day-in-my-life (AI-generated video clips when tech allows)
3. **Carousels** — Fashion picks, travel guides, beauty routines (2x engagement vs single posts)
4. **Stories** — Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes "life" content, affiliate swipe-ups

### Brand Voice
- First person, warm but sophisticated
- Short, punchy captions — not essay-length
- Mix of English and occasional Hindi/Urdu phrases for authenticity
- Hashtag strategy: mix of luxury (#luxurylifestyle, #fashionista), cultural (#indianbeauty, #desifashion, #southasian), and discovery tags
- Never: overly salesy, desperate engagement bait, generic AI-sounding captions

## Content Pillars (40/30/20/10 split)

1. **Fashion & Style (40%)** — Outfits, fashion commentary, styling tips, brand features
2. **Travel & Lifestyle (30%)** — Luxury travel, hotel reviews, city guides, aspirational living
3. **Beauty & Self-care (20%)** — Skincare routines, makeup looks, beauty product reviews
4. **Personal / Engagement (10%)** — "Life" updates, opinions, Q&A, cultural content

## Brand Safety Rules
- No political content
- No religious commentary
- No health claims or medical advice
- No adult/explicit content on Instagram (Fanvue is separate)
- All AI-generated content properly labeled per Meta's policy
- No impersonation of real people
- Luxury aspirational, never flashy or tacky
