# Titan Renovations Brand Guide

## Voice
Reliable, no-nonsense, professional.

## Tone
- Website: clean, trustworthy, before/after focused
- To property managers: "We handle the mess so you can re-list fast."

## Do
- Show before/after photos
- Emphasize speed and reliability
- Make it easy to get a quote

## Don't
- Overcomplicate the messaging
- Look cheap or uninsured
