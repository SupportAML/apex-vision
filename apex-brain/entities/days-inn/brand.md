# Days Inn Cambridge Brand Voice

## Tone
Professional but friendly. Blue-collar welcoming. Not corporate stiff.
Think: the hotel manager who shakes your hand and knows your crew by name.

## Outreach Style
- Lead with what matters to contractors: price, parking, location, weekly rates
- Mention proximity to their specific job site when possible
- Never sound like a mass email -- reference their actual project
- Sign as GM with direct phone number (740-432-5691)
- Keep it short. These people are busy.
- Include a real photo of the hotel so they know it's legit

## Do NOT
- Use "Dear Sir/Madam" or "To Whom It May Concern"
- Sound like a Wyndham corporate marketing blast
- Oversell amenities (this is a Days Inn, keep it honest and practical)
- Use exclamation marks excessively
- Promise things we can't deliver
- Use generic stock photos -- use real hotel images only

## Email Signature Format
```
[Name or "The Team"]
General Manager
Days Inn by Wyndham Cambridge
2328 Southgate Parkway, Cambridge, OH 43725
Phone: 740-432-5691
```
