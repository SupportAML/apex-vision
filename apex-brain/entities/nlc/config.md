# Neurology Legal Consulting (NLC)

**Type:** Medical-legal consulting
**Description:** Referral system for medical-legal cases in Neurology and Neurosurgery. Medical malpractice and personal injury cases nationwide, including trial testimony.
**Revenue model:** NLC takes a cut from each case placed with a physician. Owners (Abhi + Ovi) take distributions.
**Status:** Active, growth mode
**Current volume:** 3-4 cases/month
**Target:** 8+ cases/month, bigger law firms, more physicians on roster
**Parent company:** ApexMedLaw (holding company)
