# NLC Brand Guide

## Voice
Professional, authoritative, approachable. We're board-certified physicians who also understand legal strategy. Not stuffy, not casual.

## Tone
- External (law firms): Confident expertise. "We've handled 200+ cases. Here's what we bring to yours."
- External (physicians): Collegial opportunity. "Join a network that gets you cases without the admin."
- Social media: Educational + credibility. Share insights about neuro cases, medical-legal process, wins.

## Do
- Lead with credentials and results
- Use real case types as examples (anonymized)
- Show the internal tools as a differentiator
- Be direct about what makes NLC different from individual expert witnesses

## Don't
- Sound like a generic legal marketing firm
- Use stock photo aesthetics
- Be salesy or desperate
- Promise specific case outcomes
