# A2Z Equity

**Type:** Investment club
**Description:** Pooled investment vehicle. Friends and family contribute capital, Abhi and Ovi find and vet opportunities.
**Co-founders:** Abhi Kapuria, Ovais Inamullah
**Status:** Active, growth mode
**Capital raised:** ~$250k in year one
**Target:** Significantly exceed that in year two

## How It Works
- Source opportunities (e.g., MicroVentures SpaceX allocation at $15k min)
- Vet and present to investor group
- Pool capital and invest together
- Report returns

## Growth Levers
- Find more high-quality opportunities faster
- Create polished investor pitch decks
- Expand beyond friends and family to new investor channels
- Build track record with transparent reporting
