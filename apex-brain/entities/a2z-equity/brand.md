# A2Z Equity Brand Guide

## Voice
Smart money, accessible. We find the deals most people can't access alone.

## Tone
- To investors: transparent, data-driven, exciting but not hype-y
- Pitch materials: clean, professional, numbers-forward
- Updates: concise, results-focused

## Do
- Lead with the opportunity, not the ask
- Show deal flow quality and vetting process
- Be transparent about risks and timelines

## Don't
- Promise returns
- Sound like a hedge fund when you're an investment club
- Use jargon that alienates non-finance people
