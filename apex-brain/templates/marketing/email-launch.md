# Product Launch Email Sequence Template (8 Emails)

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Emails 1-4)

### Email 1: Announcement (2 weeks before)
**Subject:** Something new is coming...
**Goal:** Build anticipation, get on the radar

### Email 2: The Problem (10 days before)
**Subject:** Why [current solution] isn't working anymore
**Goal:** Agitate the problem your launch solves

### Email 3: The Solution Tease (7 days before)
**Subject:** What if you could [desired outcome] without [pain point]?
**Goal:** Preview the solution without revealing everything

### Email 4: Early Access (3 days before)
**Subject:** You're on the early access list — here's your advantage
**Goal:** Exclusivity, reward engaged subscribers

## Phase 2: Launch (Emails 5-7)

### Email 5: It's Live (Launch day)
**Subject:** [Product] is here — [benefit in 5 words]
**Goal:** Drive traffic to sales page, create urgency

### Email 6: Social Proof (Day 2)
**Subject:** [Number] people already [action] — here's what they're saying
**Goal:** FOMO + validation from early adopters

### Email 7: FAQ / Objections (Day 4)
**Subject:** Your top [number] questions about [product], answered
**Goal:** Remove remaining barriers to purchase

## Phase 3: Closing (Email 8)

### Email 8: Last Chance (Day 7)
**Subject:** Final hours: [offer/bonus] expires at midnight
**Goal:** Urgency-driven close, deadline enforcement
