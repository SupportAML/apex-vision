# Welcome Email Sequence Template (5 Emails)

## Email 1: Welcome (Send immediately)
**Subject:** Welcome to [Business] — here's what to expect
**Goal:** Set expectations, deliver promised lead magnet

```
Hi [First Name],

Welcome! [One sentence about what they signed up for.]

Here's [the thing you promised — guide, checklist, access link].

Over the next few days, I'll share:
- [Value point 1]
- [Value point 2]
- [Value point 3]

Quick question — what's your biggest challenge with [topic]?
Just hit reply. I read every response.

[Sign-off]
```

## Email 2: Quick Win (Day 2)
**Subject:** The fastest way to [achieve result]
**Goal:** Deliver immediate value, build trust

```
Hi [First Name],

Most [audience type] struggle with [common problem].

Here's the fix — takes 5 minutes:

[Step 1]
[Step 2]
[Step 3]

I've seen this [specific result] for [client type].

Tomorrow I'll share [teaser for next email].

[Sign-off]
```

## Email 3: Story + Credibility (Day 4)
**Subject:** How [Client/We] went from [problem] to [result]
**Goal:** Build authority through storytelling

```
Hi [First Name],

[Short origin story or client case study]

The turning point was [specific insight or action].

Here's what changed:
- Before: [pain state]
- After: [desired state]
- Timeline: [how long it took]

The key lesson: [actionable takeaway they can apply].

[Sign-off]
```

## Email 4: Objection Handling (Day 6)
**Subject:** "But what about [common objection]?"
**Goal:** Address the #1 reason people don't buy

```
Hi [First Name],

The most common thing I hear is: "[objection]"

Fair concern. Here's the reality:

[Address objection with data/story/logic]

[Social proof — quote or stat that neutralizes the objection]

[Reframe: what it actually costs to NOT take action]

[Sign-off]
```

## Email 5: Soft CTA (Day 8)
**Subject:** Ready to [desired outcome]?
**Goal:** Convert to next step (call, purchase, signup)

```
Hi [First Name],

Over the past week, you've learned:
- [Recap value point 1]
- [Recap value point 2]
- [Recap value point 3]

If you're serious about [achieving result], here's the next step:

[Clear CTA — book a call, start trial, purchase]

No pressure. But if [pain point] is costing you [quantified cost],
the math is simple.

[Sign-off]

P.S. [Urgency element — limited spots, time-bound offer, or scarcity]
```

## Sequence Metrics to Track
| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Open rate | 40-60% (welcome), 25-35% (rest) |
| Click rate | 3-7% |
| Reply rate | 2-5% |
| Unsubscribe | < 1% per email |
