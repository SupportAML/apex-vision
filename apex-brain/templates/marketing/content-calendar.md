# 30-Day Content Calendar Template

## Content Pillars (5-Day Rotation)
1. **Educational** — Teach, how-to, tips, frameworks
2. **Behind the Scenes** — Process, team, culture, day-in-life
3. **Social Proof** — Results, testimonials, case studies
4. **Engagement** — Questions, polls, debates, community
5. **Promotional** — Products, services, offers, CTAs

## Weekly Template

| Day | Pillar | LinkedIn | Instagram | Twitter/X |
|-----|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Mon | Educational | Long post | Carousel | Thread |
| Tue | BTS | Story + post | Reel | Tweet |
| Wed | Social Proof | Case study | Testimonial graphic | Quote tweet |
| Thu | Engagement | Poll/Question | Story poll | Hot take |
| Fri | Promotional | Offer post | CTA post | Promo tweet |
| Sat | — | Rest or repurpose | Reel (evergreen) | Retweet best |
| Sun | — | Rest | Story recap | Rest |

## Repurposing Workflow
1. Write one long-form piece (blog post or LinkedIn article)
2. Extract 5 key points → 5 social posts
3. Create 1 carousel from the key points
4. Record 1 short video (60s) on the topic
5. Pull 3 quotes for standalone graphics

## Hook Formulas
- "Most people get [X] wrong. Here's why:"
- "[Number] [things] I learned from [experience]"
- "Stop [common mistake]. Do [better approach] instead."
- "The secret to [result]? It's not what you think."
- "I [impressive result] in [timeframe]. Here's the playbook:"

## Hashtag Strategy
- 3-5 niche hashtags (< 100K posts)
- 2-3 medium hashtags (100K-500K posts)
- 1-2 broad hashtags (500K+ posts)
- 1 branded hashtag
