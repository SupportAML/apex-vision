# Lead Nurture Email Sequence Template (6 Emails)

Rule: 70% value, 20% story, 10% promotion.

## Email 1: The Big Mistake (Week 1)
**Subject:** The #1 [industry] mistake that costs [audience] [amount]
**Goal:** Position expertise, create problem awareness

## Email 2: Framework/Method (Week 2)
**Subject:** Our [number]-step [method name] for [result]
**Goal:** Share proprietary framework, build authority

## Email 3: Case Study (Week 3)
**Subject:** How [client type] achieved [specific result] in [timeframe]
**Goal:** Social proof through detailed story

## Email 4: Common Myths (Week 4)
**Subject:** [Number] myths about [topic] that are holding you back
**Goal:** Reframe thinking, challenge assumptions

## Email 5: Behind the Scenes (Week 5)
**Subject:** What actually happens when you [work with us / use product]
**Goal:** Reduce uncertainty, show the process

## Email 6: The Invitation (Week 6)
**Subject:** Is [desired outcome] on your radar for [timeframe]?
**Goal:** Convert — soft ask with clear next step
