# Growth Strategy - AI Influencer
schedule: weekly review

## Objective
Grow Instagram following from 0 to 150K in 12 months through organic content, strategic engagement, and targeted paid promotion.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-3) — Target: 10K followers

### Organic Growth Tactics
- Post 1 feed image + 1 Reel daily (7 days/week)
- Use 20-30 hashtags per post (mix of reach and niche)
- Engage with 50+ accounts daily in target niches (like, comment, follow)
- Respond to every comment and DM within 4 hours
- Cross-post Reels to TikTok for additional discovery

### Content Strategy
- 60% Reels (algorithm favors video for new accounts)
- 30% Carousels (highest save rate)
- 10% Single image posts (editorial quality)

### Hashtag Tiers
- High reach (1M+): #fashion, #luxurylifestyle, #ootd, #beauty
- Mid reach (100K-1M): #indianfashion, #desistyle, #luxurytravel, #fashioninspo
- Niche (10K-100K): #indianbeauty, #southasianfashion, #aimodel, #virtualinfluencer
- Branded (build over time): #[charactername], #[charactername]style

### Key Milestones
- Day 1: Account live, first 9 posts (grid aesthetic)
- Week 2: First Reel, establish posting rhythm
- Month 1: 1K-3K followers, 30+ posts, engagement rate baseline set
- Month 3: 10K followers, eligible for more features

## Phase 2: Acceleration (Month 4-6) — Target: 50K followers

### Paid Growth
- Instagram Ads: $200-300/mo on Reel promotions
- Target: South Asian diaspora in US, UK, UAE, Canada (18-35, female, fashion/beauty interests)
- Boost top-performing organic Reels only (proven content)
- Target CPA: $0.05-0.15 per follower

### Collaboration
- Engage with other AI influencer accounts (comment, duet Reels)
- Feature mentions with human micro-influencers in adjacent niches
- Brand collaborations that expose to new audiences

### Content Evolution
- Introduce recurring series (weekly "style pick", monthly "travel diary")
- Start behind-the-scenes content (drives Fanvue curiosity)
- User-generated content: repost followers styling similar looks

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12) — Target: 150K followers

### Paid Growth Scale-up
- Instagram Ads: $500-800/mo
- Expand targeting: add international fashion audiences
- Retarget engaged users for Fanvue conversion

### Authority Building
- Get featured in fashion/AI media outlets
- Pitch to PR agencies covering virtual influencers
- Apply to Instagram Creator Marketplace
- Pursue verification (blue check) at 100K+

### Multi-platform Expansion
- TikTok (repurpose Reels)
- Pinterest (fashion boards drive long-tail discovery)
- YouTube Shorts (additional revenue stream)

## Weekly Review Checklist
- [ ] Follower growth rate (target: 5-10% week-over-week in Phase 1)
- [ ] Engagement rate (target: 5%+)
- [ ] Top 3 performing posts this week (why did they work?)
- [ ] Bottom 3 performing posts (what to avoid?)
- [ ] Hashtag performance (which drove most discovery?)
- [ ] DM/comment sentiment (positive, negative, brand inquiries?)
- [ ] Ad spend vs. follower growth (if running ads)
