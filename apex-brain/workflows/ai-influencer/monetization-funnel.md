# Monetization Funnel - AI Influencer
schedule: weekly

## Objective
Convert Instagram followers into revenue through brand deals, affiliate marketing, and Fanvue subscriptions.

## Revenue Streams (by priority)

### Stream 1: Fanvue Subscriptions (target: 60% of revenue)
**Setup:**
- Create Fanvue creator account
- Set subscription tiers: $9.99/mo (basic), $24.99/mo (premium), $49.99/mo (VIP)
- Basic: exclusive lifestyle photos, behind-the-scenes
- Premium: personalized content, early access, DM responses
- VIP: custom image requests, priority DM, exclusive sets

**Funnel:**
- Instagram bio link → Fanvue
- Stories teasers ("exclusive set dropping on Fanvue tonight")
- Reels with "full version on my page" hooks
- DM automation for interested followers

**Metrics:**
- Conversion rate target: 1-2% of followers → subscribers
- Churn target: < 15% monthly
- ARPU target: $20-30/subscriber

### Stream 2: Brand Deals (target: 25% of revenue)
**Outreach strategy:**
- At 10K followers: reach out to 20 micro-brands per month (Indian fashion, beauty, jewelry)
- At 50K: register on Creator Marketplace, accept inbound
- At 100K: hire management or use agent platform

**Pricing:**
- 10K followers: $200-500/post
- 50K followers: $1K-3K/post
- 100K followers: $3K-8K/post
- 200K+ followers: $5K-15K/post

**Target brand categories:**
- Indian fashion designers and luxury brands
- Beauty/skincare (especially brands targeting South Asian skin)
- Travel/hospitality (luxury hotels, airlines)
- Jewelry and accessories
- Tech/lifestyle products

### Stream 3: Affiliate Marketing (target: 15% of revenue)
**Setup:**
- Amazon Associates (fashion, beauty, lifestyle)
- RewardStyle / LTK (fashion affiliate network)
- Individual brand affiliate programs (Sephora, Net-a-Porter, etc.)
- Commission range: 5-15% per sale

**Integration:**
- Linktree in bio with tracked links
- Product tags in posts
- "Shop my look" carousels
- Story swipe-ups with affiliate links

## Automation via Apex Brain
- Auto-generate monthly brand outreach emails using email-outreach skill
- Track all revenue streams in dashboard
- Auto-flag when follower milestones are hit (trigger pricing updates)
- Weekly analytics digest: revenue by stream, top-performing affiliate products, subscriber growth

## Skills Used
- email-outreach
- competitor-analysis
- social-media-content

## KPIs
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Revenue per follower (RPF)
- Fanvue subscriber count and churn rate
- Brand deal close rate
- Affiliate conversion rate
- Cost of content production vs. revenue (target: 10:1 ROI by Month 9)
