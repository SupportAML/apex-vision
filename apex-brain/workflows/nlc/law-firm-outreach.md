# Law Firm Outreach Pipeline - NLC
schedule: weekly

## Objective
Identify and contact law firms that handle medical malpractice and personal injury cases, get them to use NLC for expert witnesses.

## Schedule
Weekly (Tuesday)

## Steps
1. **Research** - Find target law firms via web search, legal directories, LinkedIn
2. **Qualify** - Check firm size, practice areas, case volume
3. **Draft** - Create personalized outreach (email + LinkedIn message)
4. **CRM** - Add to lead pipeline, tag as qualified
5. **Follow-up** - Automated sequence with case study attachments
6. **Meeting** - When interested, schedule demo of case management dashboard

## Skills Used
- email-outreach
- competitor-analysis

## Tools Used
- tools/send_email.py
- tools/browser_agent.py
