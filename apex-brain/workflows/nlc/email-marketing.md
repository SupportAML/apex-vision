# NLC Email Marketing Campaign
schedule: weekly

## Overview
Weekly email marketing campaign targeting law firms specializing in medical malpractice and personal injury.
AI agent scrapes prospects, analyzes their needs, and generates personalized outreach emails.

## Skills Used
- email-outreach

## Target Audience
- Medical malpractice law firms
- Personal injury attorneys
- Law firms handling neurology-related cases

## Steps

## Step 1: Prospect Discovery
Scrape law firm directories and public databases for potential clients.
Focus on firms that handle medical malpractice, personal injury, and neurology cases.

## Step 2: Prospect Qualification
Use Claude to analyze each firm's profile, practice areas, and recent cases.
Score relevance (0-100) based on likelihood of needing expert witness services.

## Step 3: Email Generation
Generate personalized cold outreach emails for each qualified prospect.
Emails should reference the firm's specific practice areas and how NLC can add value.
Follow the brand voice: confident expertise, "200+ cases reviewed."

## Step 4: Dashboard Review
Push all generated emails to the marketing dashboard for marketer approval.
Each email sits in the queue until approved or rejected.

## Step 5: Send
After approval, send emails via Resend with CAN-SPAM compliance.
Track opens and responses.
