# NLC Social Media Content
schedule: daily

## Overview
Daily social media content creation for Neurology Legal Consulting across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and Instagram.

## Platforms
- LinkedIn (primary — thought leadership for law firms and physicians)
- Twitter/X (industry commentary, case insights, quick tips)
- Instagram (brand awareness, team, behind-the-scenes, case studies)

## Skills Used
- social-media-content
- email-outreach

## Content Pillars (5-Day Rotation)
1. **Educational** — Medical-legal insights, neurology case analysis
2. **Behind the Scenes** — Expert witness process, case preparation, team
3. **Social Proof** — 200+ cases completed, client outcomes (anonymized)
4. **Engagement** — Medical-legal industry questions, polls, debates
5. **Promotional** — Services, physician recruitment, law firm partnerships

## Steps

## Step 1: Topic Research
Research current medical-legal trends, recent neurology case developments, and NLC's active priorities. Check the entity goals and recent outputs.

## Step 2: Draft Content
Generate 3 platform-specific posts:
- LinkedIn: 1500-2500 chars, professional, hashtags, thought-leadership angle
- Twitter/X: Under 280 chars, punchy, 2-3 hashtags
- Instagram: Visual-first caption, storytelling, 5-10 hashtags, include image prompt

## Step 3: Visual Concept
For Instagram, generate a detailed image prompt. For LinkedIn, suggest a visual concept if appropriate.

## Step 4: Review
Push all content to the marketing dashboard for marketer approval. Auto-approve if confidence > 90%.

## Step 5: Post
After approval, post to each platform via the auto-post dispatcher.

## Step 6: Track
Log engagement metrics after 24 hours.
