# Marketing Intelligence Scanner
schedule: daily

## Overview
Daily automated scan for the latest marketing tools, techniques, algorithm changes,
and industry trends across all entity verticals.

## Skills Used
- competitor-analysis
- market-report

## Steps

## Step 1: Landscape Scan
Research the latest developments in:
- AI marketing tools and platforms
- Social media algorithm changes (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, TikTok)
- Content marketing techniques and trends
- Email marketing best practices
- Industry-specific marketing news for all entities

## Step 2: Analysis
Evaluate each finding for relevance to our entities.
Categorize as: tool, technique, algorithm change, trend, or competitor move.
Score actionability.

## Step 3: Report
Generate a structured intelligence report with 5-8 key findings.
Each finding includes recommendation and relevant entities.

## Step 4: Notify
Push report to marketing dashboard intelligence tab.
Send digest to marketing team if high-priority findings exist.
