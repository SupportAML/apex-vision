# Porcupine Edu Social Media Content
schedule: daily

## Overview
Daily social media content for Porcupine Edu — educational content platform.

## Platforms
- Instagram (visual learning content, infographics, community)
- Twitter/X (educational tips, quick facts, engagement)

## Skills Used
- social-media-content

## Content Pillars
1. **Educational** — Learning tips, study techniques, interesting facts
2. **Behind the Scenes** — Content creation process, team, research
3. **Social Proof** — Student success stories, community highlights
4. **Engagement** — Quiz questions, polls, "did you know" threads
5. **Promotional** — New courses, merch, platform features

## Steps

## Step 1: Topic Selection
Pick educational/engaging topic based on content pillar rotation.

## Step 2: Draft Content
- Instagram: Visual-friendly caption, educational tone, image prompt for infographic
- Twitter/X: Quick facts, engaging questions, thread-friendly

## Step 3: Review
Push to marketing dashboard for approval.

## Step 4: Post
Auto-post after approval.
