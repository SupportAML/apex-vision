# SEO Content Pipeline - Porcupine Edu
schedule: weekly

## Objective
Create and publish SEO-optimized educational articles to capture top Google rankings for porcupine-related keywords.

## Schedule
3 articles/week

## Target Keywords
- porcupine facts
- porcupine habitat
- types of porcupines
- are porcupines dangerous
- porcupine quills
- porcupine diet
- baby porcupine (porcupette)
- porcupine vs hedgehog

## Steps
1. **Keyword research** - Identify next best keyword to target (search volume, difficulty, intent)
2. **Outline** - Create article structure optimized for featured snippet capture
3. **Draft** - Write 1500-2500 word article with headers, images, FAQs
4. **SEO** - Add meta title, description, schema markup, internal links
5. **Publish** - Deploy to website
6. **Index** - Submit to Google Search Console
7. **Track** - Monitor ranking position weekly

## Skills Used
- seo-content
- frontend-design

## Output
- Articles saved to outputs/porcupine-edu/content/
