# Titan Renovations Social Media Content
schedule: daily

## Overview
Daily social media content for Titan Renovations — Instagram focus for visual project showcases.

## Platforms
- Instagram (project photos, before/after, craftsmanship showcase)

## Skills Used
- social-media-content

## Content Pillars
1. **Educational** — Renovation tips, material guides, cost breakdowns
2. **Behind the Scenes** — Active projects, crew at work, process shots
3. **Social Proof** — Completed projects, client testimonials, before/after
4. **Engagement** — Design polls, "which finish?" questions
5. **Promotional** — Services, seasonal offers, free estimates

## Steps

## Step 1: Topic Selection
Choose based on content pillar rotation and any active projects.

## Step 2: Draft Content
Instagram: High-impact visual caption, craftsmanship tone, image prompt for project showcase.

## Step 3: Review
Push to marketing dashboard for approval.

## Step 4: Post
Auto-post after approval.
