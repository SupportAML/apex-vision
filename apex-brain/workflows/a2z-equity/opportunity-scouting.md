# Investment Opportunity Scouting - A2Z Equity
schedule: weekly

## Objective
Find high-quality investment opportunities to present to the investor group.

## Schedule
Weekly (Wednesday)

## Steps
1. **Scan** - Check MicroVentures, AngelList, Republic, StartEngine, equity crowdfunding platforms
2. **Filter** - Minimum criteria: proven traction, reasonable valuation, accessible minimum investment
3. **Research** - Deep dive on top 3 opportunities (financials, team, market, risks)
4. **Pitch** - Create investor pitch deck for each viable opportunity
5. **Present** - Queue pitch for Abhi and Ovi review
6. **Distribute** - On approval, send to investor group via email

- competitor-analysis
- trend-scout
