# ApexMedLaw Email Marketing Campaign
schedule: weekly

## Overview
Weekly email marketing campaign targeting hospitals and healthcare systems.
AI agent discovers potential hospital clients, analyzes their needs, and generates customized outreach.

## Skills Used
- email-outreach

## Target Audience
- Hospitals and healthcare systems
- Hospital risk management departments
- Healthcare legal departments
- Medical centers needing expert witness services

## Steps

## Step 1: Prospect Discovery
Scrape hospital directories (CMS, AHA) and healthcare databases.
Focus on hospitals in states with active medical-legal markets.

## Step 2: Needs Analysis
Use Claude to analyze each hospital's profile, size, specialty departments.
Identify potential need for medical-legal consulting services.
Score relevance (0-100).

## Step 3: Email Generation
Generate personalized emails addressing each hospital's specific situation.
Highlight how ApexMedLaw's specialty branches can serve their needs.
Professional, authoritative tone.

## Step 4: Dashboard Review
Push emails to marketing dashboard for marketer approval.

## Step 5: Send
Send approved emails with proper compliance footers.
