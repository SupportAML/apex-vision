- Twitter/X (industry updates, medical-legal news)

2. **Behind the Scenes** — How the specialty branch model works
3. **Social Proof** — Branch performance, physician network growth
4. **Engagement** — Industry discussions, healthcare-legal intersection topics
5. **Promotional** — New specialty launches, partnership opportunities

Generate LinkedIn and Twitter posts tailored to the corporate brand voice.
