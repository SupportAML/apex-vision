# Barber Onboarding - Club Haus
schedule: on-demand

## Objective
Standardized onboarding process for new barbers joining Club Haus.

## Trigger
New barber hired by Zeus

## Steps
1. **Documents** - Generate onboarding packet (welcome letter, policies, schedule expectations, compensation structure)
2. **Square setup** - Add barber to Square POS with their own booking profile
3. **Training** - Provide brand guidelines and customer experience standards
4. **Social** - Add barber's profile to website team page
5. **Access** - Grant dashboard access (Club Haus entity only)

## Output
- Onboarding docs saved to outputs/club-haus/onboarding/
