# Club Haus Social Media Content
schedule: daily

## Overview
Daily social media content for Club Haus barbershop — Instagram-first with Twitter/X support.

## Platforms
- Instagram (primary — visual showcase of cuts, shop vibes, community)
- Twitter/X (local engagement, promotions, community)

## Skills Used
- social-media-content

## Content Pillars
1. **Educational** — Grooming tips, style guides, hair care advice
2. **Behind the Scenes** — Shop life, barber spotlights, setup/process
3. **Social Proof** — Client transformations, reviews, before/after
4. **Engagement** — Style polls, "which cut?" questions, local community
5. **Promotional** — New services, booking reminders, specials

## Steps

## Step 1: Topic Selection
Pick a topic based on the content pillar rotation. Prioritize visual/lifestyle content.

## Step 2: Draft Content
- Instagram: Eye-catching caption, warm/inviting tone, 5-10 hashtags, image prompt
- Twitter/X: Short, casual, local flavor

## Step 3: Review
Push to marketing dashboard for approval.

## Step 4: Post
Auto-post after approval.
