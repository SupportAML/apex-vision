## LinkedIn Post

**Content:**
Sunday reflection: After 6 years of residency at Duke and 25+ published papers, I've learned that the best plastic surgery outcomes happen when patients are fully educated participants in their care.

Too often, our industry focuses on the "after" without explaining the "during" and "between." 

Real talk about rhinoplasty:
• Swelling peaks at day 3-4, not day 1
• You'll see 70% of your result at 6 weeks, 90% at 6 months
• The bridge refines faster than the tip
• Your nose will feel "weird" for months (this is normal)

When patients know what to expect, they're calmer, heal better, and get superior results. Education isn't just good medicine — it's good business.
